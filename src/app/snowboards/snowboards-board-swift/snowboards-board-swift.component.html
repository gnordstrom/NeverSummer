Swift

Freeride

Designed with a nod to the past and an eye toward the future the Swift is ready for anything. Virtually unsinkable, the Swift’s forked tail, tapered shape and wide early rise nose joined with our Original Rocker Camber Profile allows it to glide through the tightest trees and ride on top of the deepest fluff but this ain’t just the ultimate powder surfer. By adding a long Carbon X, our Vario Power Grip Sidecut, mid firm flex and extensive damping we’ve made a stable board for shredding hardpack conditions too. Capped off with Aluminum Tip/Tail Protection and our premium Carbonium Topsheet the Swift is a work of art. Give the mountain a Swift kick!
