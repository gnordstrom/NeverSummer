Twenty Five Split

Freeride

The Blaho brothers have been handcrafting splitboards in our Denver, Colorado factory since 2000 and their innovation continues today. The Twenty Five Split is equipped with the Ripsaw Rocker Camber Profile for power and control in variable conditions. A unique “bat tail” allows it to sink, elevating its elongated defined nose to knife through the deepest pow. Our new Hextech Topsheet with the commemorative 25th Anniversary logo has the Twenty Five Split looking as badass as she performs.
