MEET RAJAT... I grew up in Colorado Springs, but have been living in Jackson, Wyoming for the last 21 years. I’ve been with NS since the beginnings. I knew I had found the answer ever since I saw that small stack of decks in the corner of the Summit Snowboards Factory. I’ve ridden and represented these boards ever since, all over the world, and hope to do so as long as I can. 

RAJAT'S FAVORITE NS BOARD IS... The 158 West. Snappy, rails like crazy, floats in the pow, everything I’m looking for.
